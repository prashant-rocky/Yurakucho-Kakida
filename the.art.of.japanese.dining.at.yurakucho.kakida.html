The Art of Japanese Dining at Yurakucho Kakida

When it comes to Japanese cuisine, few experiences rival the delicate balance of flavor, presentation, and tradition. At Yurakucho Kakida, we believe that dining is more than just enjoying a meal—it is an exploration of culture, craftsmanship, and passion.

Our chefs, led by Chef Kazuhiro Kakida, carefully curate every dish with precision and respect for seasonal ingredients. From melt-in-your-mouth Toro sashimi to the refined artistry of our Omakase sushi course, each plate is a reflection of Japan’s culinary heritage.

Our visitors often describe their time with us as “an unforgettable sushi experience” and “a true journey of flavor.” The blend of authenticity, creativity, and warm hospitality ensures that every guest leaves with a smile.

Nestled in Shibuya, Tokyo, Yurakucho Kakida provides more than just exquisite dishes—it offers an atmosphere of comfort and sophistication. Whether you are visiting for a casual lunch, an evening of fine dining, or a celebration with loved ones, every visit becomes a cherished memory.

Why Dine at Yurakucho Kakida?

  • Authentic Craftsmanship – Every dish is prepared with precision by skilled chefs led by Chef Kazuhiro Kakida.
  • Fresh Seasonal Seafood – From Toro sashimi to Uni sushi, only the finest ingredients are chosen daily.
  • Elegant Atmosphere – A refined yet welcoming setting, perfect for both casual and special occasions
  • Cultural Experience – Dining here is not just a meal, but a journey into Japanese tradition and hospitality.
  • Guest Favorites – Visitors consistently describe their meals as unforgettable, praising both flavor and presentation.

About Us

Yurakucho Kakida is a fine-dining Japanese restaurant in Tokyo, offering authentic sushi and seasonal seafood in an elegant setting where tradition meets modern flavor.
